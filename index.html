Research Project

Optimizing the Early Detection of Dementia by Tracking the Progression of Parkinsons Disease Using Deep Learning and Computer Vision

  • The project focuses on longitudinal modeling of Parkinson’s disease progression and dementia risk using PPMI MRI and clinical data. Cohorts of PD patients with and without cognitive impairment, along with healthy controls, are curated and labeled, with imaging and clinical data organized in a unified Python-based pipeline. Deep learning models are developed to learn spatio-temporal progression patterns from multi-timepoint MRI and to estimate dementia risk using progression representations and clinical features. Model performance is evaluated using Accuracy, F1-score, and ROC-AUC, with Attention Mechanism applied to support model interpretability.
